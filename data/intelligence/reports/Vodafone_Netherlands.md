## 1. COMPANY INTELLIGENCE

### 1.1 Company Overview

**VodafoneZiggo** is a Dutch telecommunications joint venture formed in January 2017 between Vodafone Group plc (50%) and Liberty Global Ltd (50%). The company provides fixed, mobile, and integrated communication and entertainment services to consumers and businesses across the Netherlands.

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Headquarters** | Utrecht, Netherlands |
| **CEO** | Stephen van Rooyen (since September 2024) |
| **CFO** | Ritchy Drost (since 2017) |
| **Employees** | ~5,734 (before 2025 restructuring) |
| **Q3 2025 Revenue** | EUR 990 million (quarterly) |
| **Annual Revenue (2024)** | EUR 4.1 billion (~$4.5B USD) |
| **Q3 2025 EBITDA** | EUR 447 million (quarterly) |
| **Total Customers** | 7+ million |
| **Mobile Customers** | 5+ million |
| **Fixed-Line Customers** | ~4 million |
| **Network Coverage** | Gigabit speeds to 7.5 million homes |
| **2 Gbit/s Rollout** | 7 million households by end of 2025 |
