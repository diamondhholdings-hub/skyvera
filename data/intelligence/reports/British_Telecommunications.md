### 3.2 Why BT Chose CloudSense

**Problem Statement:**
BT was facing challenges with its legacy platform comprising multiple independent applications. This hindered:
- Coordination and sharing of customer data between marketing, sales, field engineers, and customer services teams
- Sales efficiency and customer experience
- Time-to-market for new products (15-18 months)

**CloudSense Differentiation:**
- Salesforce-native architecture (BT's strategic CRM platform)
- Telco-specific CPQ capabilities for complex B2B product configuration
- Handles multi-site enterprise quotes efficiently
- Pre-built integrations with Salesforce Industries
- TCS implementation partnership providing telco expertise

**Executive Validation (Hena Jalil, CIO of Business, BT Group):**
> "A key pillar of our digital strategy is platform thinking. We decided that if we were going to create a sales platform we needed an anchor system. Salesforce is our anchor system."
