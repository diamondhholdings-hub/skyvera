## 1. COMPANY INTELLIGENCE

### 1.1 Company Overview

**PostNL Holding BV** is the largest postal and parcel delivery company in the Netherlands, and a major logistics provider across the Benelux region (Belgium, Netherlands, Luxembourg). The company is the successor to the postal division of the former Dutch state-owned TNT N.V., following the spin-off in 2011.

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Headquarters** | The Hague, Netherlands |
| **CEO** | Pim Berendsen (since April 15, 2025) |
| **CFO** | Linde Jansen (since April 15, 2025) |
| **CIO** | Willem-Jan van Tongeren (since September 2024) |
| **Employees** | ~30,000-35,000 (fluctuates seasonally) |
| **FY2024 Revenue** | EUR 3,252 million (~$3.5B USD) |
| **Q1 2025 Revenue** | EUR 783 million |
| **FY2024 Normalized EBIT** | EUR 53 million (margin: 1.6%) |
| **Stock Exchange** | Euronext Amsterdam (PNL.AS) |
| **Total Parcels Delivered (2024)** | 371 million |
| **Market Position** | #1 in Netherlands parcel/mail delivery |

**Note:** PostNL is not a traditional telecommunications company, making it a non-core vertical for CloudSense. However, their complex B2B commercial operations and Salesforce commitment align well with CloudSense capabilities.

### 3.2 Why PostNL is a CloudSense Customer

**PostNL's Salesforce Journey (Documented):**

PostNL has been a significant Salesforce customer since 2011 when the Parcel division deployed Sales Cloud. Key milestones include:

1. **2011:** Sales Cloud deployment for Parcel division
2. **2013:** Had 750+ applications running; began rationalization
3. **2016:** Deployed Community Cloud-based employee app for 23,000+ mail carriers
4. **Ongoing:** "1PostNL" vision standardization across all divisions

**Executive Quote (Marcel Krom, Former CIO):**
> "We want customers to engage with us as one company; at the moment we are too many companies within one... With Salesforce, we can innovate faster, integrate smarter and collaborate better."

**CloudSense Fit:**
- Complex B2B commercial operations requiring sophisticated quoting
- Multi-channel sales (direct, partners, digital)
- Product/service bundle configuration
- Contract lifecycle management needs
- Native Salesforce requirement aligns with PostNL's platform strategy

### 7.2 Detailed Risk Analysis

**MEDIUM RISK - Financial Pressures:**

PostNL's financial situation creates budget scrutiny risk:
- FY2024 EBIT declined to EUR 53M (from EUR 92M in 2023)
- EBIT margin compressed to 1.6% (from 2.9%)
- Operating loss in Q1 2025 of EUR 15M
- Labor costs increasing (+EUR 31M organic costs in Q1 2025)
- Dividend reduced from EUR 0.09 to EUR 0.07

**Mitigation Actions:**
1. Provide detailed ROI analysis showing cost savings from CloudSense
2. Position as enabler of efficiency targets
3. Quantify automation benefits in labor cost context
4. Offer flexible payment/pricing if needed

**MEDIUM RISK - New Leadership:**

Both CEO (Pim Berendsen) and CFO (Linde Jansen) are new as of April 2025. CIO Willem-Jan van Tongeren joined September 2024.

**Mitigation Actions:**
1. Schedule introduction meeting with new CIO immediately
2. Prepare executive briefing on CloudSense value delivered
3. Align messaging with "Breakthrough 2028" strategy
4. Position as strategic partner, not just vendor

**LOW-MEDIUM RISK - Non-Core Vertical:**

PostNL is not a telecommunications company, CloudSense's core market.

**Mitigation Actions:**
1. Demonstrate logistics/postal sector knowledge
2. Reference other non-telco customers (utilities, media)
3. Show CPQ/OMS value is industry-agnostic
4. Develop logistics-specific use cases
