## COMPANY OVERVIEW

### Business Profile

Advance Publications, Inc. is one of America's largest privately held media conglomerates, owned by the Newhouse family (Donald Newhouse and heirs of Samuel Irving Newhouse Jr.). Founded in the 1920s, the company has evolved from a regional newspaper chain into a diversified media and investment holding company.

**Core Business Units:**
- **Conde Nast** - Premium magazine publishing (Vogue, The New Yorker, Wired, Vanity Fair, GQ, Architectural Digest)
- **Advance Local** - Regional newspaper and digital media network (12+ local news websites, 30+ newspapers)
- **American City Business Journals** - Business news publications
- **Stage Entertainment** - European theater operations
- **The IRONMAN Group** - Endurance sports events
- **Turnitin** - Academic integrity software ($1.75B acquisition in 2019)
- **1010data** - Big data analytics platform

**Major Investment Holdings:**
- Reddit: ~30% ownership stake (major shareholder)
- Charter Communications: ~13% ownership
- Warner Bros. Discovery: 3.97% ownership (~98M shares)

## STRATEGIC CONTEXT

**Market Position:**
- One of the largest private media companies in the US

- Advance Local: 52M+ monthly readers across 200+ publications
