NS Solutions Corporation is a leading Japanese IT services company operating as a subsidiary of Nippon Steel Corporation. The company provides comprehensive IT solutions including:

- **Digital Transformation (DX):** AI, IoT, 5G, big data analytics
- **Information Security:** Cybersecurity solutions and consulting
- **IT Infrastructure:** Network design, server management, IT lifecycle support
