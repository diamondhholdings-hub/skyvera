## 1. COMPANY OVERVIEW

### Corporate Profile

**Legal Name:** Masergy Communications, Inc.
**Parent Company:** Comcast Business (acquired October 2021)
**Ultimate Parent:** Comcast Corporation (NASDAQ: CMCSA)
**Headquarters:** 2740 Dallas Parkway, Suite #100, Plano, TX 75093
**Founded:** 2000
**Employees:** ~544 (December 2025, -11% YoY)
**Global Presence:** 4 continents (North America, Europe, Asia)
**Customer Base:** 1,400+ enterprise customers across 100+ countries

**Confidence Level:** HIGH - Multiple authoritative sources confirm

### Business Description

Masergy Communications is a software-defined networking services company and pioneer in managed SD-WAN, SASE, UCaaS, and CCaaS solutions. Following the 2021 acquisition by Comcast Business, Masergy operates as an integrated division serving mid-market to large enterprises with complex global networking requirements.

**Core Service Lines:**
- **Managed SD-WAN** - Software-defined WAN with Fortinet FortiGate/FortiSASE technology
- **Unified Communications (UCaaS)** - Cisco Webex-based voice/video/messaging
- **Cloud Contact Center (CCaaS)** - Omni-channel customer engagement platform
- **Managed Security** - SASE, threat detection, cloud workload protection, CASB

**Industry Verticals Served:**
- Healthcare
- Financial Services & Banking
- Manufacturing
- Entertainment & Broadcasting
- Hospitality
- Technology Services
