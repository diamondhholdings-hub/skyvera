Business Challenges

High - Advertising Revenue Growth: Spotify is aggressively growing its advertising business to diversify beyond subscriptions. Need efficient ad sales configuration and pricing tools.
High - Podcast Monetization: After significant podcast investment, Spotify needs to optimize monetization workflows and partner revenue sharing.

Technical Pain Points

Medium - Multi-Market Operations: Operating in 180+ markets with varying pricing, licensing, and regulatory requirements. CPQ complexity is significant.

Opportunity Areas

Medium - Profitability Focus: After years of growth-first strategy, Spotify achieved profitability in 2024. Maintaining margins while growing requires operational efficiency.

Competitive Landscape

Based on industry analysis and market intelligence:

  • Salesforce CPQ (Medium threat): Standard CPQ platform. Counter with media-specific customizations and existing integration.
